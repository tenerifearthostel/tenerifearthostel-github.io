About Us

and about what you can expect

Welcome to Tenerife Art Hostel, the perfect blend of budget-friendly accommodation and artistic inspiration! Our hostel, located in the heart of Santa Cruz de Tenerife, is a feast for the eyes, decorated with a diverse range of art pieces. Relax in our comfortable rooms and make use of our spacious kitchen and beautiful terrace. Immerse yourself in the cultural richness of the city and meet fellow travelers from all over the world. Whether you're a budget-conscious traveler or an art lover, Tenerife Art Hostel has everything you need for a memorable stay. Book now and join our creative community!
